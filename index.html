Focus Mode

Block apps until
you hit your goal

Focus Mode uses iOS Screen Time to shield selected apps until you've walked enough for the day. The moment your goal is met, apps unblock automatically.

  • Pick any app using the system App Picker — no manual entry
  • Apps re-block automatically each midnight for the new day
  • Premium: block entire categories and set a custom time window
  • No app selection data ever leaves your device
